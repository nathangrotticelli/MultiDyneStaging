KVM-1000

A High–Quality, Long–Haul, Fiber-Optic Transport Solution for RGB–HV and DVI–D.

The KVM–1000 is designed to transport DVI computer graphics while extending computer control via USB. With integrated USB hubs on both sides of the link, the KVM–1000 goes far beyond a standard remote destop allowing the easy addition of other peripherals such as printers, scanners or even web cams. All on two multimode fibers.

The video transport section supports a high– quality DVI–D interface and will significantly increase the transmission distance limitations of high resolution DVI video and computer graphics over multimode fiber. The KVM–1000 will automatically collect EDID information from the display and adjust accordingly to give you plug and play convenience.

The KVM–1000 system will transport virtually any VESA–compliant DVI signal from 640x480 VGA up to and including 1920x1200 WUXGA. Standard 24–pin DVI connectors are provided at each side of the link. HDMI is supported via an included adapter cable.

Each end also incorporates an integrated USB 2.0 hub for all of your peripherals for easy connection convenience and less desktop clutter. Do you use high–powered devices on your USB Bus? The RX unit supplies up to 500mA per output and solves that problem, too. All USB connectors are standard “A” type.

As with all Multidyne products, rack kits are available for rack mounting up to three units in a single RU. And for your optical connections, choose between ST, FC, SC or LC for easy integration into your fiber backbone.

There are many KVM over fiber products on the market today at many different price points. The KVM–1000 answers the call for a low–priced yet full featured KVM solution for multimode fiber. If you have installed singlemode fiber or need to cover longer distance requirements, please look at our KVM–6000 series of KVM extenders.

The KVM–1000: DVI and USB at a price that will surprise you, complete with an industry leading 7–year warranty.

  • Fully uncompressed to 1920 x 1200
  • Supports DVI-D to WUXGA
  • Supports HDMI
  • Supports HDCP
  • Self-Detecting EDID
  • Throw-down or rack
