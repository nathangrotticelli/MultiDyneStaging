RGB-5000-SXGA

Synchronous, Single Fiber, Digital, RGB and VGA Fiber Optic System.

  • Transport RGB, XVGA, SVGA and UXGA signals plus various configurations of H and V sync over ONE fiber
  • 1280 x 1024 @ 60 Hz
  • Horizontal gen-lock for a fully synchronous and jitter free high resolution video transport. Video and sync jitter reduced to as low as 4 - 5 nano seconds
  • State-of-the-Art Design, supports a higher quality image at higher resolutions
  • Data throughput 2.5 Gbps. Most systems have a 1.25 GB/S data throughput with high compression
  • Horizontal Frequency 15-130 KHz
